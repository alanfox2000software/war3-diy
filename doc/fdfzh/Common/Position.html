Position定位

定位是UI-Frame的重要继承,war3使用4:3比例的屏幕,坐标系统因此也是4:3的.

坐标系的原点0.0/0.0在屏幕的左下角小地图,0.8/0.6在右上角.Frame的大小(Size)也是使用这些坐标值划分的,转换为像素大小需要一定的数学运算.

可以将Frames看做具有定位和大小的矩形.另外每个Frame使用一个FramePoint作为Frame扩展方向的划分.
FramePoint如下:

  • LEFT只会在x轴向右扩展x->.
  • RIGHT只会在x轴向左扩展x<-.
  • CENTER可以在4个方向扩展<->
  • TOP只会在y轴向下扩展
  • BOTTOM只会在y轴向上扩展.​

使用不同的FramePoint可以得到不同结果.

自定义创建的Frame不可以离开游戏屏幕,如果有部分在游戏屏幕外,将会得到一个不规则的.TEXT-Frame会裁剪多余的文字 .BACKDROP和Buttons中的图片将会自动缩小等.
SimpleFrames则可以脱离这些限制

One can free Frames from that 4:3 limitation by creating them for a different Parent then GAME_UI, in Reforged such a Parent could be ("ConsoleUIBackdrop",0). Through that don't comes with a price. Using ("ConsoleUIBackdrop",0) as parent pushes them to a lower Layer, below SimpleFrames. One also could use BlzGetFrameByName("Leaderboard", 0) or BlzGetFrameByName("Multiboard", 0). This are above SimpleFrames but one has to create them first with the none Frame api. Your custom Frames can also be a child's child of that other Parents, the 4:3 Limitation is still gone.

FDF中定义的Frame可以定位,有以下几种定位几种:

  • SetPoint FramePoint-Own, FrameName, FramePoint-FrameName, xOffset , yOffset,
  • SetAllPoints,
  • Anchor FramePoint, x, y, (仅用在String/Texure)​
SetPoint和Anchor可以使用如下的Points:
  • TOPLEFT
  • TOP
  • TOPRIGHT
  • LEFT
  • CENTER
  • RIGHT
  • BOTTOMLEFT
  • BOTTOM
  • BOTTOMRIGHT
SetPoint可以看做是`DzFrameSetPoint(self, FramePoint-Own,DzGetFrameByName(FrameName, 0), FramePoint-FrameName, x, y)`
SetAllPoints则等价于`DzFrameSetAllPoints(self, parent)`.
Anchor等价于`BlzFrameSetPoint(self, point, parent, point, x, y)`
