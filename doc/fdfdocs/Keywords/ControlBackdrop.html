ControlBackdrop

格式 ControlBackdrop FrameName,

例子 ControlBackdrop "ButtonBackdropTemplate",

作用: Mimic Position and Size of the parent Frame. It is the texture displayed when the parent-Frame is enabled Is the name of the frame beeing used, The is also most times declared right below the control line. Most advanced Frame have such a thing or a custom named version of it.

应用: MENU, POPUPMENU, BUTTON, GLUEBUTTON, TEXTBUTTON, GLUETEXTBUTTON, SCROLLBAR, SLIDER, CONTROL, TEXTAREA, EDITBOX, GLUEEDITBOX, GLUECHECKBOX, CHECKBOX
